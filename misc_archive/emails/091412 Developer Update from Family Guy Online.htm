Hey Family Guy Online Citizen,

Thanks for playing Family Guy Online during our Open Beta! We appreciate the feedback you've given us and we’re hard at work on making improvements to the game. We're excited about the changes coming up over the next few months. You'll see big improvements to our combat system, much faster load times, and better performance on different systems and most browsers.

As part of our efforts to balance the game, we noticed there are a lot of quests to complete in order to earn the first Tier of Citizenship from Mayor West. In an effort to optimize the playing experience, we're going to separate the tiers into smaller, bite-sized tiers and add some more combat quests. Note that we are NOT wiping the database. You will keep all of your characters, your cash, your clams, your XP, your level, your skills and your items. However, we do want to give you a heads up that we need to reset the quest logs. This means you will have to play through the quests again, but if you are all leveled and powered up, you can blaze through it quickly.

Thanks again for helping us make Family Guy Online better. If you have any questions regarding these changes, please send an email to support@familyguyonline.com.

Cheers,
